08 What to do

Four priority moves to turn KBank's strong branded footprint into broader answer ownership across the largest prompt pools.

01

Build comparison hubs that AI can cite directly

KBank's clearest structural gap is in cashback, no-fee, points, and side-by-side card-selection prompts. Comparison pages should be explicit, current, and machine-citable.

02

Expand fund education for the largest non-card prompt pool

Fund-selection and investment prompts represent the biggest measured bucket, but visibility is only mid-range. Prioritise ETF, Thai ESG, REIT, and risk-based fund explainers.

03

Strengthen beginner and eligibility guidance

These prompts often surface KBank, but owned-page citation capture is weak. Add clearer explainers on approval, requirements, minimum payment, application steps, and fit.
