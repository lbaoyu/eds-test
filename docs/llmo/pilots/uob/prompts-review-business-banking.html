UOB Business Banking Prompt Quality Review

This review evaluates prompt quality across the business banking set using a conservative standard: retain strong prompts unchanged, recommend light edits only where they clearly improve precision, and merge only true duplicates. Suggestions are included only where they represent a real, defensible quality gain.

Review Standard

Retain a prompt unchanged when

  • It reflects a plausible customer question.
  • It already expresses a clear comparison, selection, or discovery intent.
  • No proposed wording change materially improves clarity or usefulness.

Recommend a light edit when

  • The prompt is valid, but a small wording adjustment improves precision.
  • The original intent can be preserved fully.
  • The revised wording is clearly better, not just different.

Recommend a merge when

  • Two prompts are effectively the same question.
  • Keeping both adds no meaningful segment, product, or use-case coverage.
Important: words such as best or good are not treated as automatic defects. Prompts are assessed on realism, specificity, intent clarity, and distinct coverage.

Reviewed Findings

Keep as-is Strong general comparison prompt

What are the best digital business banking options in Singapore?

This prompt is already valid. It expresses a realistic comparison need and does not require rewriting.

Keep as-is Strong segment-based prompts

What is a good business account for SMEs in Singapore? Which business bank account is best for startups in Singapore? Which UOB business account is better for an established SME?

These prompts should remain separate. Each covers a different business segment and adds distinct value.

Light edit Broad generic comparison prompt

What is the best business bank account in Singapore?

This prompt is valid as written. A wording change is optional rather than necessary. If a tighter version is preferred, the revised form should clarify the decision frame without changing the intent.

Optional alternative: Which business bank accounts in Singapore are best suited to different business needs?

Merge One clear duplicate pair

Which UOB business account is best for companies with international payments? Which UOB business account is best for cross-border payments?

These two prompts cover the same branded payment need and are the clearest merge candidates in the overlap area.

Suggested merged version: Which UOB business account is best for cross-border or international payments?

Prompts That Should Stay Separate

Similar topic area alone is not sufficient reason to merge prompts. The following prompts should remain distinct.

Prompt Reason to keep separate
Which bank is best for small business banking in Singapore? Provider-level framing; broader than a single account-selection prompt.
Which Singapore business bank account has low fees? Specific cost-led decision criterion.
What is the best business account for USD transactions in Singapore? Distinct currency-specific operational need.
Which Singapore bank is best for multi-currency or cross-border business banking? Broader international banking scope than a USD-only or payment-specific question.
What is the best bank for trade finance for SMEs in Singapore? Trade finance is a separate product and decision category.
