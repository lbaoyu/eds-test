C2 The opportunities

Two to act on now, plus the housekeeping that keeps the data honest.

1 · Win business-account & SME discoveryManual opportunityUnbranded · discovery
Evidence
Business-account answers are heavily grounded — 1,010 citations — but only 2.6% are UOB-owned; the rest are publishers and competitors (Reddit, Wise, Airwallex, OCBC, Statrys). Account Opening is High-popularity at 46.1 visibility with no owned page among 333 citations, and both dark prompts sit here. Brand Presence + Data Insights + URL Inspector.
Why it matters
This is where prospects choose a bank. Demand already exists and the model already answers confidently — it simply isn't choosing UOB's content as the source. The score is recoverable because the answer is being given; what's missing is owned authority. This is the pattern "answers are sourced, but not from us."
Action
Build owned, AI-readable business-account content that answers the discovery questions directly — an Account-Opening decision page (documents, eligibility, foreign-owned, timeline), a UOB-vs-others comparison, and fees / rates / multi-currency — and earn coverage in the SME-publisher arena where the citations currently land.
How we'll know
Owned citation share on business-account topics rises above 2.6%; Account Opening and Comparison visibility climb out of the 40s–60s; the two dark prompts return a non-zero score.
2 · Defend the service coreDefend the winBranded · service
Evidence
Branded · service questions average 74.8 visibility with zero dark prompts, 100% share of voice on several core topics, 1st-position answers and neutral/positive sentiment. UOB owns 41.9% of the citations on these questions. Brand Presence + Data Insights + URL Inspector.
Why it wins
It isn't luck. Specific, UOB-named questions meet owned, well-structured, AI-readable service pages (card customer-service, fee-waiver, card-services), so the model grounds answers on UOB's own content. That owned grounding is the asset that produces the high score.
What erodes it
Those pages going stale or being restructured, competitors starting to be cited on the same topics, or sentiment slipping. Visibility is re-scored continuously, so a won topic is not permanently won.
How to keep it
Keep the cited service pages accurate and current; treat them as the content-quality template; monitor weekly for visibility or sentiment drift and for new competitor citations; and extend the same page structure to adjacent service journeys before gaps open.
How we'll know
Branded · service visibility holds in the mid-70s or above; owned citation share stays around 40%+; sentiment stays non-negative; no service topic drifts dark.

Remove

Enable
