KBank is the most-cited bank in the Thai set. Investment-fund discovery is the open gap.

How AI assistants see Kasikornbank, over three weeks across 98 questions. KBank is the most-cited bank in the comparison set and ranks first in share of voice on every topic — but it leads only thinly in a fragmented field, and outside card promotions the answers are grounded on third-party aggregators, not KBank's own pages. C2 The opportunities

One to win, one to defend, one technical fix — plus the housekeeping that keeps the data honest.

1 · Win investment-fund discoveryManual opportunityUnbranded · discovery
Evidence
Investment answers are heavily grounded — 893 citations — but only 3.2% are KBank-owned. Fund selection & returns is the highest-demand topic at 22.5 visibility with 10 dark prompts. The cited sources are the aggregator yuanta mutualfund (201), my-best, wealthmagik and finnomena, plus rival SCBAM (74). KBank's own fund arm, K-Asset, appears only as an earned domain (75). Brand Presence + Data Insights + URL Inspector.
Why it matters
This is where prospective investors choose where to put their money. Demand already exists and the model already answers confidently — it simply isn't choosing KBank's content as the source. KBank has the products (K-Asset funds) but no AI-readable owned authority for fund-selection questions. This is the pattern "answers are sourced, but not from us."
Action
Build owned, AI-readable fund content that answers the discovery questions directly — fund-selection decision pages (by goal, risk and horizon), a fund comparison, and RMF / SSF / Thai ESG tax-fund guides — on kasikornbank.com and kasikornasset.com, and earn coverage in the fund-aggregator arena (my-best, finnomena, wealthmagik) where the citations currently land.
How we'll know
Owned citation share on investment topics rises above 3.2%; fund selection visibility climbs out of the 20s; the 16 dark investment prompts return non-zero scores; KBank's share of voice on fund topics moves past the high single digits.
2 · Defend & extend the card coreDefend the winCards
Evidence
Card questions average 47.4 visibility, rising to 86.4 on branded prompts, with first-position answers, no negative sentiment, and KBank owning 14.9% of the citations. Owned promotion pages — cashback, dining, shopping, card-comparison — are the cited sources. Brand Presence + Data Insights + URL Inspector.
Why it wins
Specific card questions meet owned, well-structured, AI-readable promotion pages, so the model grounds answers on KBank's own content. That owned grounding is the asset that produces the high score.
What erodes it
Promotions are time-bound — a citation to an expired promo page is a live risk as campaigns rotate. Competitors are close behind (KTC holds 7.3% share of voice on rewards, UOB 6.5%). Visibility is re-scored continuously, so a won topic is not permanently won.
How to keep it
Keep cited promotion pages current as campaigns rotate; treat them as the content-quality template for the fund build; monitor weekly for visibility drift and new competitor citations; and extend the same owned-page structure to investment journeys before gaps open.
How we'll know
Card visibility holds in the high 40s or above; owned citation share stays around 15%; sentiment stays non-negative; no card topic drifts dark.
3 · Pre-render the owned estateTechnical · high leverageAccess
Evidence
47 of 82 measured owned pages render under half their content to an AI crawler; the mean is 48% content visibility and gain ratios reach 88×. Affected pages include card-promotion, foreign-exchange-rate and property pages — content the assistants would otherwise cite. Access (content visibility).
Why it matters
Where content renders client-side after the crawl, the crawler sees a fraction of the page, so it cannot cite it — even when KBank has the best answer. This silently caps both opportunities above: building fund content won't pay off if the crawler can't read it.
Action
Server-side render or pre-render the priority owned pages so the raw HTML carries the content. It is a content-free engineering fix that compounds with opportunities 1 and 2 — start with the cited card pages and the fund pages built for opportunity 1.
How we'll know
Content visibility on priority pages climbs toward 90%+; gain ratios fall toward 1×; citations on pre-rendered pages rise.
4 · Housekeeping & enablementDirect fixes
Enable CDN
Supply CDN logs to light up the Agentic (Access) and Referral (Impact) layers — so crawl health becomes visible and the citation wins can be tied to on-site sessions and conversions.
Fix tracking
Complete the Market-Tracking competitor list — KTC, UOB and ttb register zero there yet appear with real mentions and citations in Brand Presence, so the tracked-competitor configuration is incomplete.
Tidy library
Remove the leftover blank template prompt set (generic US/GB "Card Services" rows that ship with the tool) so the export contains only KBank's 98 curated prompts.
