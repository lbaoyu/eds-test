HKTB Prompt Revision – Second Pass

Balancing natural prompts, source-grounded answers, and a reasonable branded layer

The goal of this revision is to improve the likelihood of generating meaningful Brand Presence metrics in LLMO by combining three prompt patterns: natural discovery prompts, source-seeking planning prompts, and a selective branded / official-source layer.

Revision logic: not every prompt should name HKTB or Discover Hong Kong, but a small and intentional share should. That branded layer is most useful for official guidance, itinerary, visitor information, and "what should I know / where can I find" type prompts, where branded wording is still realistic and more likely to produce source-grounded answers and measurable citations.

Recommended Pattern

How the 200-prompt set should feel after revision

Balanced branded share

The revised set should not become heavily branded. Instead, the set should preserve a broad natural layer while introducing a measured branded layer where official guidance is realistic.

Prompt Pattern Example Purpose in Brand Presence
Natural broad Is Hong Kong good for a first trip to Asia? Measures general visibility and whether HKTB appears naturally in broad answer spaces.
Source-seeking planning Where can I find trusted information for planning a Hong Kong trip? Improves the chance of grounded answers and more actionable citation data.
Branded official What does Discover Hong Kong recommend for a 3-day Hong Kong itinerary? Tests direct HKTB / Discover Hong Kong source capture and whether official pages are cited.
Outcome Sought

Why this balance matters

  • Natural prompts help measure broad market visibility and traveler intent coverage.
  • Source-seeking prompts help increase the chance of citations and therefore more usable Brand Presence data.
  • Selective branded prompts help test whether HKTB / Discover Hong Kong is being recognized and cited as an authoritative source.
  • Together, this should create more meaningful mentions and citation metrics in the Brand Presence dashboard to guide targeted optimisation work.
