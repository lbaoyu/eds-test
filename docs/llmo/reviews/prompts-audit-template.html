Section 2

Scoring guide

Apply the same standard throughout the review so your scores remain consistent.

Score 2

Strong

The item is well designed, strategically useful, and ready to keep with little or no change.

Score 1

Needs Revision

The idea is valid, but the execution is weak. Rewrite, narrow, rebalance, or reclassify.

Score 0

Weak

The item is low value, too vague, too broad, duplicative, or poorly structured.

Issue Types

Use tags like overlap, too broad, too vague, duplicate, misclassified, missing coverage, or localization gap.
