What are the "work together" options — from now to the long run?

The options differ by how tightly the workflow is brought together inside the Adobe experience.

Available today

Combined

Semrush and Adobe LLM Optimizer run as separate products, but they can already work together in a practical customer workflow.

  • Teams can use Semrush to identify prompts, topics, and discoverability opportunities
  • Those prompts can be exported from Semrush, lightly refined, and uploaded into LLM Optimizer
  • LLM Optimizer then supports monitoring, optimization, and impact measurement
Near-term direction

Connected

Semrush functions are brought into the LLM Optimizer product experience, so customers do more of the workflow inside LLMO itself.

  • Less dependence on separate handoffs between tools
  • More Semrush-driven intelligence surfaced directly inside LLM Optimizer
  • A stronger in-product flow from discovery and prioritization into action
Long-term vision

Unified

A closed-loop Adobe workflow managed through one Adobe tool, where insight, action, and measurement are part of a single end-to-end system.

  • One Adobe experience across visibility discovery, optimization, and business impact
  • Tighter connection to broader Adobe content and experience workflows
  • A full closed-loop model for AI-driven brand visibility
Combined means separate tools working together today. Connected means Semrush capabilities brought into LLM Optimizer. Unified is the longer-term end state: one Adobe tool across the full workflow.
