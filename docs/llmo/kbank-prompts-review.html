KBank Prompt Optimization Review

AI brand presence prompt rewrite and quality improvement recommendations.

Goal Improve prompt quality for AI brand presence testing.
Method Rewrite prompts to be more natural, specific, and measurable.
Output Original vs optimized prompt tables with short change notes.

Review Goals

  • Improve realism: make prompts sound more like natural user queries.
  • Clarify intent: add helpful context such as use case, risk level, or decision criteria.
  • Reduce ambiguity: avoid overly broad prompts that may trigger generic AI responses.
  • Fix hygiene issues: correct typos, casing, spacing, and inconsistent formatting.
  • Preserve brand testing value: keep branded prompts explicit where brand association is the objective.
