They're prescriptive and brand-safe — not the generic advice you'd get by asking ChatGPT to "write some content," which is what several competitors' suggestions amount to.

The flagship

Optimize at Edge: fixes live in minutes, reversible in one click

The hardest part of GEO isn't knowing what to change — it's shipping it past an engineering backlog. Optimize at Edge deploys AI-visibility fixes at the CDN layer, so the origin CMS never changes: no authoring work, no dev cycle, live in minutes, and fully reversible with one-click rollback.

It's CMS-agnostic — it works on any front-end architecture, not just Adobe's, across Fastly, Akamai, Cloudflare, CloudFront, and bring-your-own-CDN. And it targets only agentic traffic: human visitors and SEO crawlers see your site unchanged.

Deployment has two axes — what you change and where it lands. Brand Visibility is one of the few platforms that can cover all four cells:

On the common objection — is this cloaking? No. Pre-rendering simply restores content parity, recovering JavaScript-hidden content that human visitors already see; content opportunities reorganise or summarise what's on the page rather than fabricating claims. Supported opportunities today include Recover Content Visibility, LLM-Friendly Summaries, Relevant FAQs, Simplify Complex Content, Table of Contents, Multimedia Transcript Summaries, and Enrich Product Detail Pages for Commerce.

Prove it

The only stack that closes the loop to revenue

A visibility score is upstream; the business case is downstream. This is where Adobe's ownership of the analytics stack becomes the decisive edge. Native integration with Adobe Analytics and Customer Journey Analytics connects AI visibility to engagement, conversion, and revenue — and Real-Time CDP carries it into audiences and activation.
