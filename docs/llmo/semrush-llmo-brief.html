What are the "work together" options — from now to the long run?

The differences are about how tightly Semrush intelligence and Adobe execution are brought together in the customer workflow.

Available today

Combined

Two separate products, one practical workflow.

  • Use Semrush to discover prompts, topics, and competitive visibility opportunities
  • Export those prompts from Semrush, make a small refinement, and upload them into LLM Optimizer
  • Use LLM Optimizer for monitoring, optimization, and impact measurement
Near-term direction

Connected

A more integrated experience inside LLM Optimizer.

  • Semrush capabilities are surfaced directly inside the LLM Optimizer product experience
  • Customers do less manual export-import handoff between separate tools
  • The flow from discovery and prioritization into action becomes more seamless
Long-term vision

Unified

One Adobe solution for the full closed-loop workflow.

  • A single Adobe environment across discovery, prioritization, optimization, and measurement
  • Not just integrated screens, but one end-to-end operating model
  • A full closed-loop system for AI-driven brand visibility and business impact
Combined means separate tools working together today. Connected means Semrush capabilities brought into LLM Optimizer. Unified means one Adobe tool across the full closed-loop workflow.

How to position these options with customers

The right framing depends on whether the customer is asking about what is available now, what deeper integration looks like, or what the long-term Adobe end state could become.

Combined
Available today. Position this as a recommended workflow using two products together: Semrush for prompt discovery and intelligence, then a quick export-refine-upload handoff into LLM Optimizer for optimization and measurement.
Connected
Next level of integration. Position this as Semrush functionality being brought into the LLM Optimizer experience, reducing manual tool switching and making more of the workflow happen inside LLMO.
Unified
Ultimate Adobe end state. Position this as a single Adobe solution for the full closed-loop workflow from visibility discovery and prioritization through optimization and business impact measurement.
