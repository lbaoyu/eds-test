Review Standard

Keep as-is Light edit only when clearly helpful Merge only true duplicates

Retain unchanged when

  • The prompt reflects a plausible customer question.
  • The selection or comparison intent is already clear.
  • No wording change would materially improve answer quality.

Recommend change only when

  • A small edit improves precision without changing intent.
  • Two prompts are functionally duplicates.
  • The adjustment improves the library in a meaningful way, not just stylistically.
Important: words such as best, good, or better are not treated as automatic defects. Prompts are judged on realism, clarity, and distinct coverage.

Which UOB business account is best for companies with international payments? Which UOB business account is best for cross-border payments?

These two prompts are the clearest true duplicates in the set. Both are UOB-specific account-selection prompts focused on the same international payment need.

Recommended consolidated version: Which UOB business account is best for cross-border or international payments?
