B1 How the mix is structured

Broad hub pages

Homepage, Travel guide, Attractions, Events, and Food & drink give the trial strong coverage across high-volume travel discovery and planning topics.

High-intent leaf pages

Accommodation, Getting around, Visa information, Muslim travel, and The Peak create more focused answer targets and make it easier to assess page-level movement.

In short: this is a practical first cohort for Edge deployment because it covers the main visitor journey stages without spreading the trial too thin.

Recommended prompt changes

  • Reduce broad/open-ended prompts that naturally collapse into homepage and attraction pages.
  • Add page-explicit prompts for getting around, visa information, Muslim travel, and The Peak.
  • Remove noisy prompts that are too narrow, too current-event specific, or poor for repeatable comparison.
  • Standardise prompt style using English, full-question format, and stable visitor intent.

Why this helps

  • Reduces over-crediting broad hub pages.
  • Creates clearer page cohorts for before/after comparison.
  • Makes lower-coverage pages measurable in a fairer way.
  • Improves confidence that any lift comes from Edge deployment rather than prompt mix.

Prompts to reduce or rewrite

Current prompt pattern Why adjust it
What are the best things to do in Hong Kong for first-time visitors? Too broad and likely to over-credit homepage or attractions.
What should I do in Hong Kong on my first visit? Broad intent with weak page-level attribution.
