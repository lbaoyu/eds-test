08 What to do

Three priorities for this quarter, then the full prioritised opportunity list. P0 = act now; P1 = next 90 days; P2/P3 = scope and plan.

01

Fix the leaks before pouring more water in

14.5% of all bot traffic hits a 403 or 404. Resolving robots.txt over-blocking, restoring missing URLs, and consolidating redirect chains recovers ~110,000 hits per period with zero new content. Lowest effort, highest yield.

02

Lift visibility on the top 10 landing pages

Ten URLs receive ~33,500 bot hits per period at 18% average visibility. Moving them to ≥60% compounds across every downstream metric — citation rate, mentions, share of voice — because these are the pages AI agents actually read.

03

Build "Where to Stay" and "HK vs Singapore" hubs

The two themes HKTB is being most decisively out-cited on. Both are decision-stage queries with high commercial intent. Authoritative AI-readable hubs would address ~50% of the top trending citation gaps.

Strategic posture
HKTB has the brand authority. What it lacks is the answer surface area.

The GEO mandate for the next two quarters: convert authority into surface area — by fixing technical drag, lifting page-level readability, building decision-stage content hubs, and earning placement on the third-party properties already shaping the destination's AI answer.
